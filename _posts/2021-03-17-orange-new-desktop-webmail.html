--- title: "Updated data for Orange.fr’s new desktop webmail" date: 2021-03-17 --- Orange.fr is currently rolling out [a new desktop webmail](https://messagerie.orange.fr/mail-orange/index.html). And I am afraid it is not a good news. Out of the 179 HTML and CSS features tested, the new webmail **only supports 95 features** (from previously 156). In our [software combination Support Scoreboard](/scoreboard/), Orange.fr’s desktop webmail is falling from the 6th position to the **25th position**. Among the most glaring regressions are CSS properties like background longhand properties (`background-image`, `background-cover`, …), `border-radius`, `display:flex` or CSS units (like `rem`, `vw` or `vh`). And it also filters out the `!important` keyword, making responsive emails harder to build. The webmail also drops support for responsive images with `` or `srcset`. And it also no longer supports a lot of accessibility related HTML attributes (like `lang`, `role` or any `aria` attribute).
